## Active Notes Are Notes that Translate other Peoples Thoughts into Your Own

Note taking is not just about writing things down to be forgotten, it is about taking Smart Notes that can be used to further your thinking and start linking thoughts together.

Vicky Talks about using the QEC system when reading to extract out the important information. Question, Evidence and Conclusions.

Questions represent the question the author is answering with their literature. It may be a implied question or directly stated in their writing.  
Evidence is the author showing how their question is relevant, and how to resolve it.  
Conclusion is the conclusive statement the author makes in summary.

## Atomic Notes

Each Question Evidence and Conclusion are their own atomic note. Using Atomic Notes, each note can be linked multiple directions to extract the information from it.

## Connected Smart Notes

With the notes, you can start linking them to similar evidence, conclusions, questions etc. This allows you to start collating information together that can lead to further inputs and outputs.
