The process of Progressive Summarization is to help make notes in your Second Brain more discoverable.

Layer 0 - The original full-length source text  
Layer 1 - Captured content, snippets from source material  
Layer 2 - Bolding keywords, phrases, sentences to help make the eye glance them quickly.  
Layer 3 - Highlighting passages among the bolded ones to make them even more noticeable.  
Layer 4 - Summarise in your own written words.
